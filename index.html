The Orchard House & Cafe - Freeport, ME

Customer had an existing oil forced hot air furnace and an electric hot water tank servicing 2 bathrooms, kitchen and laundry facilities. The customer was converting his home into an Inn & Restaurant servicing 6 full bathrooms, 1 ADA bathroom and heating a 5,000 sqft. space.

We suggested removing the existing oil & electric systems and replacing them with a Bosch high-efficiency wall mount boiler with an 80-gallon indirect hot water tank to supply hot water for the needs of the Inn. At the same time, we added a hydronic air handler for distributing the temperature controled air throughout the space as well as including a coil and American Standard condenser for central air conditioning. Due to the requests of the customer we wrapped all the existing and new ductwork with insulation.
